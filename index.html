About Me

I am an experienced Data Scientist with a demonstrated history of working on data driven solutions. I'm passionate about deep learning and try to keep up with fast paced academia & industry research. I am curious about technologies of the future and I've picked up learning about quantum computing recently.

Education:
I've completed my bachelor's and master's (B.Tech & M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering & Statistical Signal Processing from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India.

My day-to-day job typically involves end-to-end ML lifecyle (MLOps), including:

  • writing data pipelines in PySpark & SQL
  • data analysis & exploration in Python/Jupyter notebook
  • building ML models; modeling and fine tuning
  • scheduling pipelines and retraining jobs with Apache Airflow/Cron
  • containerizing & deploying models (ONNX/TF-serving etc.) on Kubernetes/Cloud infra
  • A/B test to evaluate models before deploying in production environment
  • performing model load test (QPS, Latency etc) to adhere to downstream services/SLAs
  • monitoring models performance using DataDog/Tableau
