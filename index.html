About Me

I am a experienced Data Scientist with a demonstrated history of working on data driven solutions. I am passionate about deep learning and trying to keep up with fast paced academia & industry research. Recently I've started learning about quantum computing and the immense impact it is going to have on future technologies. I guess, I am glad to be born in this era of leap frogging and paradigm shifting technologies.

Education: I've completed my bachelor's and master's (B.Tech & M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering & Statistical Signal Processing from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India.

My day-to-day job typically involves end-to-end ML lifecyle (MLOps), including:

  • writing data pipelines in PySpark & SQL
  • data analysis & exploration in Python/Jupyter notebook
  • Building ML models; modeling and fine tuning
  • scheduling pipelines and retraining jobs with Apache Airflow/Cron
  • containerizing & deploying models (ONNX/TF-serving etc.) on Kubernetes/Cloud infra
  • A/B test to evaluate models in production environment
  • monitoring models performance using DataDog/Tableau
